# UoPeople Institutional Design Guide
Version: 2.0.0 (Direct Recreation)
Objective: Prestigious, Academic, Accessible, and Authoritative.

## 1. Professional Visual Philosophy
The design must mirror the University of the People (UoPeople). It utilizes a unique "Academic Jewel" palette that stands apart from standard "Fintech Blue" or "Web3 Neons".

- **Prestige**: Deep Purple tones signify institutional weight and history.
- **Urgency (Action)**: Rose/Primary Action colors for admissions and enrollments.
- **Clarity**: High-contrast white surfaces and Athens Gray backgrounds for learning content.

## 2. Definitive Color Palette
Standardize on these exact UoPeople tones:

- **Institutional (Main)**: `#4B345E` (Deep Purple/Voodoo) - Headers, sidebar backgrounds.
- **Primary Action (CTA)**: `#E90171` (Vibrant Rose) - Enroll buttons, "Apply Now" CTAs.
- **Secondary Accent**: `#FFC627` (Amber/Yellow) - Sparse highlights.
- **Surface/Card**: `#FFFFFF` (White).
- **Page Background**: `#F4F2F5` (Athens Gray).
- **Borders**: `#E0E0E0` (Gray - 1px).
- **Text (Body)**: `#333333` (Gray-800).

## 3. Typography
- **Headings**: Montserrat (Bold, Geometric Sans-Serif). Use for all primary UI labels.
- **Body**: Roboto or Inter (Standard weight, high-readability).
- **Credits/ID**: Font-mono for numeric matriculation identifiers.

## 4. Component Standards
- **Buttons (Primary/CTA)**: `rounded-full` (Pill-shaped), `bg-[#E90171]`, `text-white`, `font-bold`, `uppercase`, `tracking-widest`.
- **Buttons (Secondary/Portal)**: `rounded-lg` (8px), `bg-white`, `border-[#4B345E]`, `text-[#4B345E]`.
- **Active Navigation**: `border-l-4 border-[#E90171]` (In Sidebar).
- **Cards**: `rounded-lg` (8px), `border border-[#E0E0E0]`, `bg-white`, subtle hover shadows.
- **Progress Bars**: `h-2 bg-[#E0E0E0] rounded-full overflow-hidden` with `bg-[#E90171]` or `bg-[#4B345E]` fill.

## 5. Navigation Strategy (Moodle/UoPeople Standard)
- **Left Sidebar**: `240px` to `264px`. Dark Deep Purple background or High-Contrast White. 
- **Top Header**: Minimalist. Search bar and User Profile.
- **Breadcrumbs**: Standardized on every content page (Home > Dashboard > [Course]).

## 6. Prohibited Elements
- ❌ **No Navy Blue**: Do not use `blue-900` or standard "Link Blue".
- ❌ **No Neons**: Avoid standard purple/violet gradients.
- ❌ **No Sharp Corners (0px)**: Use `rounded-lg` for all containers.
- ❌ **No Oversized rounded-4xl**: Use `rounded-full` ONLY for primary CTA buttons.
