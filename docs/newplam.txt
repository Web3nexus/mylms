# Strategic Expansion: MyLMS vs. Industry Leaders

This plan outlines the comparative analysis and subsequent roadmap expansion to bring MyLMS in line with (and ahead of) enterprise learning platforms like Moodle and D2L Brightspace.

## User Review Required

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The next 10 sprints focus heavily on **Engagement**, **Automation**, and **Standardization**. This will significantly increase the complexity of the "Learning" side of the platform (LMS) to match the depth of our "Institutional" side (IMS).

## Proposed Changes

### [NEW] [LMS_Feature_Comparison.md](file:///Users/vincent/Herd/mylms/LMS_Feature_Comparison.md)
Detailed table comparing MyLMS current capabilities against Moodle and Brightspace. Key gaps identified:
- **Collaboration**: Forums, Wikis, Peer Review.
- **Interoperability**: SCORM, LTI, AICC.
- **Engagement**: Gamification, Badges.
- **Automation**: Behavior-based Intelligent Agents.
- **Pedagogy**: Competency-Based Education (CBE) and Release Conditions.
