# LMS Feature Comparison: MyLMS vs. Moodle vs. Brightspace

| Feature Area | MyLMS (Current) | Moodle (Open Source) | D2L Brightspace | Gap Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **User Interface** | Modern, React-based, Sleek. | Functional but often "dated" / cluttered. | Very high-end, intuitive, mobile-first. | **Maintain lead in UI/UX.** |
| **AI Integration** | **Advanced**: AI Tutor, AI Quiz Gen (Sprint 10). | Basic: Plugin-based AI text generation. | Emerging: Generative AI for content. | **Expand AI to predictive analytics (Sprint 29).** |
| **Automation** | **Strong**: Admissions, GPA calc, Term rotation (Sprint 20). | Basic: Scheduled cleanups/emails. | **Excellent**: "Intelligent Agents" based on behavioral triggers. | **Implement behavioral triggers (Sprint 23).** |
| **Collaboration** | **Poor**: No Forums or Peer review yet. | **Excellent**: Forums, Wikis, Peer-review Workshops. | **Good**: Discussions, Group evaluation tools. | **Prioritize Forums & Groups (Sprint 21).** |
| **Content Standards** | **None**: No SCORM or LTI support. | **Excellent**: SCORM 1.2/2004, LTI 1.3, AICC. | **Excellent**: SCORM, LTI, ePub. | **Bridge with SCORM/LTI (Sprint 22).** |
| **Gamification** | **Basic**: PDF Certificates with QR (Sprint 19). | **Good**: Badges, Rank, OpenBadges support. | **Good**: Badges, awards, personalized leaderboards. | **Implement OpenBadges (Sprint 25).** |
| **Pedagogy** | **Basic**: Standard Gradebook and GPA. | **Strong**: Competency scales, Outcomes, Rubrics. | **Elite**: Competency-Based Education (CBE) paths. | **Add Competencies & Rubrics (Sprint 26).** |
| **Institutional Control** | **Excellent**: Full SIS/IMS integration (Sprints 14-18). | **Weak**: Requires extensive plugins for SIS. | **Strong**: Deep SIS integration as SaaS. | **Leverage current IMS strength.** |
| **Content Delivery** | **Good**: Modules, Lessons (Video/HTML). | **Excellent**: "Workshop" and diverse activity types. | **Excellent**: "Video Note" and "Content Experience." | **Add Release Conditions (Sprint 24).** |

## 🎯 Strategic Gap Analysis

1.  **Collaboration Hub (Critical)**: The biggest missing "Academic" feature is Discussion Forums.
2.  **Content Interoperability (Essential)**: SCORM/LTI support is needed for professional content consumption.
3.  **Behavior-Based Automation (Strategic)**: Transitioning from scheduled to behavioral triggers (Intelligent Agents).
4.  **Learning Paths (Pedagogical)**: Unlock content dynamically ("Release Conditions").
